Hello! Let me tell you a little bit about my self...

I am a Philadelphia-based graphic designer currently studying at Philadelphia University. Though I am in my third year (class of 2018) of graphic design, I have studied other areas of design including drawing, illustration, and photography, all of which can be found in my portfolio. During my time at PhilaU, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several challenging projects, all of which have significantly developed my skills as a designer. Aside from school, I also do some freelance work as well have an internship at Diona Nicole Designs in Bala Cynwood. Feel free to follow me on social media or contact me if you’d like to know more!
